Cults

Sacred and Profane: How not to negotiate with believers

Malcolm Gladwell, March 31, 2014. The New Yorker (Strong Left Bias)

Gladwell essay on a group of 1993 religious extremists portrayed by the media as a cult. An examination of why the FBI's negotiation tactics ultimately failed and ended in the death of these misunderstood religious people. Martyrdom? So it would seem.
